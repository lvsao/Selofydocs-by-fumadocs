# Getting Started with Selofy

This guide will walk you through the initial setup process.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A Shopify store (any plan)
- Admin access to your Shopify account
- Basic understanding of your store's products

## Installation Steps

### Step 1: Sign Up

Visit [selofy.com](https://selofy.com) and create your account:

1. Click "Sign Up" in the top right
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Verify your email address

### Step 2: Connect Your Store

Once logged in:

1. Navigate to **Settings** â†’ **Integrations**
2. Click **Connect Shopify Store**
3. Authorize Selofy to access your store
4. Wait for the initial sync to complete

### Step 3: Configure Basic Settings

Set up your preferences:

- **Store Information**: Verify your store details
- **Notification Preferences**: Choose how you want to be notified
- **Time Zone**: Set your local time zone
