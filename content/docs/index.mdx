Selofy is your complete solution for e-commerce success. Our documentation will help you get the most out of our platform.

Get up and running with Selofy in minutes:

2. **Connect Your Store** - Link your Shopify store
3. **Configure Settings** - Customize your experience
4. **Start Selling** - Launch your optimized store
