# Getting Started

This guide will help you set up Max AI Alt Text Optimizer and start optimizing your store images.

## Installation

### From Shopify App Store

1. Visit the [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com)
2. Search for "Max AI Alt Text Optimizer"
3. Click "Add app" and follow the installation process
4. Grant the necessary permissions

### Initial Setup

Once installed, you'll be taken to the app dashboard where you can:

1. **Review App Permissions**: The app needs access to read and update your product images
2. **Choose Your Plan**: Select from Lite (Free), Max, or Ultra plans
3. **Configure Basic Settings**: Set your preferred language and optimization method

## First Steps

### 1. Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your store's image optimization status:

- **Alt Text Coverage**: Percentage of images with alt text (from any source)
- **Max Optimized Rate**: Percentage of images optimized by Max AI
- **By Image Type**: Breakdown by Products, Collections, and Blogs
- **Recent Activity**: Latest optimization operations

### 2. Initial Store Scan

Before optimizing, scan your store to identify images:

1. Click "Scan your store images" on the dashboard
2. Choose scan types: Products, Collections, Blogs, or All
3. Wait for the scan to complete (this may take a few minutes)
4. Review the results and statistics

### 3. Basic Configuration

#### Template Settings (Optional)

Configure default templates for different content types:

- **Product Template**: `{{ product_title }} {{ variant }}`
- **Collection Template**: `{{ collection_title }}`
- **Blog Template**: `{{ article_title }}`

#### Automation Settings (Optional)

Set up automated optimization:

1. Enable automation in Settings
2. Choose scan frequency (daily/weekly)
3. Select optimization method (AI or Dynamic Format)
4. Choose content types to include
