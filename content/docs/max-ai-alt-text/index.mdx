## <Sparkles className="inline w-6 h-6" /> What is Max AI?

Max AI is a powerful Shopify app that analyzes your **product**, **collection**, and **blog** images to generate high-quality, SEO-friendly alt text descriptions.

**Save hours of manual work** while improving your store's search engine visibility and accessibility.

---

## <Target className="inline w-6 h-6" /> Quick Start

<Steps>

### <Download className="inline w-5 h-5" /> Install the App
Visit the [Shopify App Store](https://www.selofy.com/apps/maxai-alttext) and click "Add app"

### <Search className="inline w-5 h-5" /> Scan Your Store
Click **"Scan your store images"** to analyze all images across products, collections, and blogs

### <Zap className="inline w-5 h-5" /> Optimize Images
- **AI Optimize**: Let AI analyze image content for best SEO results (recommended)
- **Dynamic Format**: Use templates for consistent formatting

### <Bot className="inline w-5 h-5" /> Enable Automation
Set up automatic daily/weekly scans to keep new images optimized hands-free

</Steps>
