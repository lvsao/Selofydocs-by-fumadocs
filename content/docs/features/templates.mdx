# Dynamic Format Templates

Create consistent, brand-aligned alt text across your store using customizable templates with dynamic variables.

---

## What are Templates?

Templates allow you to define alt text patterns that automatically populate with your product, collection, or blog information. Perfect for maintaining consistency across large catalogs.

<Callout type="info">
**When to use templates**: Great for standardized products where you want consistent formatting. For unique descriptions, use AI Optimize instead.
</Callout>

---

## Available Variables

### Products
- `{{title}}` - Product title
- `{{variant}}` - Variant name (e.g., "Blue - Large")
- `{{product_type}}` - Product type/category

### Collections
- `{{title}}` - Collection title
- `{{description}}` - Collection description

### Blogs
- `{{article_title}}` - Blog post title
- `{{blog_title}}` - Blog name

---

## Template Examples

### Product Template
```
{{title}} - {{variant}} | Premium Quality
```
**Result**: "Classic Cotton T-Shirt - Navy Blue - Large | Premium Quality"

### Collection Template
```
Shop our {{title}} collection - {{description}}
```
**Result**: "Shop our Summer Sale collection - Save up to 50% on selected items"

### Blog Template
```
{{article_title}} - {{blog_title}} | Your Brand
```
**Result**: "10 SEO Tips - Marketing Blog | Your Brand"

## Setting Up Templates

<Steps>

### Navigate to Settings
Go to **Settings** from the main navigation menu

### Select Template Tab
Choose **Products**, **Collections**, or **Blogs** tab

### Insert Variables
Click variable buttons to insert placeholders into your template

### Preview Results
Check the **Sample Data** section to see how your template renders

### Save Templates
Click **Save Templates** to apply your configuration

</Steps>

---

## Best Practices

### ✅ Do's
- Keep templates concise (under 125 characters)
- Include brand name for recognition
- Use clear, descriptive patterns
- Test with sample data before applying

### ❌ Don'ts
- Don't use ALL CAPS
- Avoid special characters excessively
- Don't make templates too generic
- Don't forget to save changes

---

## Template vs AI Optimize

| Feature | Template | AI Optimize |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| **Speed** | ⚡ Instant | 2-5 sec/image |
| **Consistency** | ✅ High | Variable |
| **SEO Quality** | Good | Excellent |
| **Cost** | Free (Unlimited) | Consumes AI quota |
| **Best For** | Standard products | Unique items |

<Callout type="warn">
**Recommendation**: Use AI Optimize for maximum SEO impact. Templates are best for budget-conscious merchants or highly standardized catalogs.
</Callout>
