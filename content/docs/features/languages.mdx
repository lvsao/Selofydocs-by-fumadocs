## How It Works

### Language Detection

Max AI automatically detects your store's primary language from:
1. **Shop Configuration** - Your Shopify store's default language
2. **Product Content** - Product titles and descriptions
3. **Manual Override** - Settings → Language Preference

### AI-Powered Translation

The GLM-4.5V model generates alt text directly in your target language:
- **Native phrasing** - Not machine translation
- **Cultural context** - Appropriate for regional audiences
- **SEO optimized** - Uses local search keywords

<Callout type="info">
**Better than translation**: AI generates alt text directly in the target language instead of translating English text, resulting in more natural and culturally appropriate descriptions.
</Callout>

## Setup Language Preferences

1. Go to **Settings** → **Language**
2. Select your primary language from dropdown
3. (Optional) Choose fallback language
4. Save settings

All future AI optimizations will use your selected language.

## Multi-Store Support

Managing stores in different regions?

| Store | Language Setting | Alt Text Output |
|-------|------------------|-----------------|
| US Store | English | "Premium cotton t-shirt in navy blue" |
| FR Store | French | "T-shirt en coton premium bleu marine" |
| JP Store | Japanese | "プレミアムコットンTシャツ ネイビーブルー" |

Each store gets optimized alt text in its local language automatically.

## Template Variables

Dynamic Format Templates work in all languages:

**English Template**: `{{title}} - {{variant}} | Premium Quality`  
**Result**: "Cotton Shirt - Blue Large | Premium Quality"

**French Template**: `{{title}} - {{variant}} | Qualité Premium`  
**Result**: "Chemise en Coton - Bleu Grande | Qualité Premium"

<Callout type="warn">
**Variable names stay in English** (e.g., `{{title}}`), but the surrounding text should be in your target language.
</Callout>

## SEO Benefits

Localized alt text improves:
- **Google Image Search** rankings in target regions
- **Accessibility** for screen readers in local languages
- **User Experience** for international customers
- **Conversion rates** through better product understanding

---

## Limitations

- **Automation Language**: Uses store's default language setting
- **Mixed Content**: If product titles are in English but store language is French, AI generates French alt text based on English product data
- **Quota Usage**: Same AI quota consumption regardless of language

<Callout>
**Pro Tip**: For best results, ensure your product titles and descriptions are already in your target language before running AI optimization.
</Callout>
